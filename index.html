Project Description

This redesign of Shopee was created in Figma to fulfill the final assignment for the Human Computer Interaction course. It has a more minimalist layout yet preserves the characteristics of the Shopee marketplace application.


Shopee's signature orange color remains, but some aspects and positions that violate the Shneiderman "Eight Golden Rules" are updated to offer a better User Interface and a more comfortable User Experience.

Project Description

BajoExplorer is an app designed to introduce tourists to the beauty of Labuan Bajo, allowing them to plan trips to this stunning destination. Users can look through a variety of trip packages, ranging from solo adventures to family vacations. Users can view their trip schedules directly within the app in addition to booking these packages.


This app was developed in the form of a Figma and Axure prototype, making it a user-friendly and interactive way to explore Labuan Bajo and plan your dream vacation.

Project Description

Enter the enchanting world of Disney's 100th anniversary celebration with a captivating 3D scene created with Three.js. I've created a magical landscape featuring Disney's iconic castle and beloved characters such as Mickey and Minnie Mouse, as well as the delightful Disney Princesses.
